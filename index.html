Born in 2025. Two sisters, one vision

Our Story

A vision born
from two sisters
and one dream.

Our story began with two sisters — one creative entrepreneur, the other a yoga teacher with a deep passion for wellness and human connection. Different paths, same dream.

In 2025, we launched our very first event — not just a class, but a space. A space where people could move, discover new places, meet inspiring people and feel part of something real.

Every event we create is an invitation to pause, breathe, and remember why feeling good matters. Come as you are. Leave as part of our community.

What makes us special

Why Choose Us

We believe wellness should feel like an experience, not a chore. Here's what sets every event apart.

◇

Every detail is tailored — from the space to the energy to the people around you.

◇

Rooftops, terraces and open-air spaces that ground you the moment you arrive.

Passionate professionals bringing expertise, warmth and energy to every class.
